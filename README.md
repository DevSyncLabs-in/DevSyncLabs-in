# 🚀 About DevSyncLabs

**DevSyncLabs** is a next-generation technology learning and innovation platform built for aspiring developers, engineers, and startups.

We empower students through project-based learning by combining:

- 🌐 Full Stack Development
- 🤖 Generative AI
- ☁️ Cloud & DevOps
- 📡 IoT & Embedded Systems
- 👨‍💻 Real-World Internship Experience

> 🔥 Our mission is to make Tier-2 & Tier-3 students industry-ready through hands-on learning, mentorship, and real-world projects.
